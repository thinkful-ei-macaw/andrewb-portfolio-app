SPACED REPETITION APP

This app allows you to learn Dutch by implementing the technique of spaced repetition by translating words from Dutch to English! The app keeps track of your correct and incorrect responses. Using Linked Lists, words that you answer correctly or incorrectly are recycled so that you can learn through repetition!
